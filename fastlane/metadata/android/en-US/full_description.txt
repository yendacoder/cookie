Cookie is a mobile client for the discuit.org social platform.

Join the friendly, open, and independent community of like-minded people.
Cookie aims for feature parity with Discuit, however some features may come at a later stage as the API is not fully documented and requires reverse engineering.

What you can do now:
- Browse posts anonymously or while logged in with your existing account (home feed or personal subscriptions). Includes an in-app YouTube player and a comfortable image viewer for single and multi-image posts.
- Comment on and vote for posts and comments, and edit or delete your own posts and comments.
- Join or leave communities, mute users or communities, and hide individual posts.
- Save posts to lists (bookmarks) and create, edit, or remove your own lists.
- Check notifications.
- Edit your own profile.
- Basic mod tools: edit community settings and manage rules.
- Light/dark mode.

Features in the app that don't exist in the web version:
- Embedded animations from Tenor/Giphy in comments.
- Full-size image viewer for image post types.
- Color-coded nested comment indicators.
- Preview text post formatting before posting.
- Clickable user @mentions in text posts and comments.

What is coming later™:
- Indicators for new comments and posts since last visit.
- Browse other users' public lists.
- Image post editing.
- Additional mod tools: moderating content, banning/unbanning users, and managing the moderators list.

What is out of scope:
- Registration and password recovery.
- GIF/animation posts, images in comments, image+text post types, and search — unless Discuit adds native support for these features.
